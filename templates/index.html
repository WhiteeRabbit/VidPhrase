About the project

VidPhrase is a simple and useful web app designed to help you find exact phrases inside YouTube content much faster. Paste a YouTube link, type the phrase you are looking for, and choose whether you want to search in subtitles, comments, or the video description. The app then scans the content and shows matching results with a similarity percentage, so you can quickly see how close each match is. When you search in subtitles, VidPhrase also provides a direct timestamp, which lets you jump straight to the exact moment in the video without watching the whole thing. This makes it especially helpful for finding specific quotes, moments, or references in long videos.
